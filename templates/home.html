About Us

Founded in 2023, Alfred's Zoo has been dedicated to inspiring a lifelong appreciation of wildlife and conservation through our captivating exhibits, educational programs, and ongoing research initiatives. We strive to create a fun, engaging, and safe environment where our visitors can come face to face with a variety of fascinating animal species from all around the globe.

Our mission is to connect people with nature and instill in them a deep respect and care for the natural world. We're committed to animal welfare and conservation, and we actively participate in global efforts to protect endangered species.

Join us for an unforgettable adventure as we explore the wonders of the animal kingdom together!
